Arch Linux

Arch Linux is a lightweight and flexible distribution designed for advanced users. It follows a rolling release model and gives you full control over what gets installed. Arch is known for its simplicity, speed, and strong community documentation.

Kali Linux

Kali Linux is a security-focused distribution used for penetration testing and ethical hacking. It comes with many preinstalled tools for cybersecurity professionals. Kali is not recommended for daily use or beginners.

Ubuntu Linux

Ubuntu Linux is a beginner-friendly and stable distribution based on Debian. It is widely used for daily work, programming, and servers. Ubuntu has strong community support and regular updates.
